Ottawa South Basketball Association Logo

Logo for the upcoming OSBA Junior Boys Invitational basketball tournament. The logo features the Parliament Buildings, a representation of Ottawa, along the curved centre line of a basketball to create unity between the sport and city. The Parliament Buidlings are created using brand colours to reinforce the brand and the clock is set to 9 and 4 to represent the association's founding in 1994.
