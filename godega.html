Overview

Godega is a grocery shopping and delivery app. It allows users to browse the flyers of multiple grocey stores in their area to compare prices and get the best deals.

Key features of the app include:

  • Browse flyers
  • Compare prices between stores
  • Add and remove items in cart
  • Order grocery items for delivery
