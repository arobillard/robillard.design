Quidditch Canada Jersey 2016

Jersey design worn by Quidditch Canada's National team at the 2016 IQA Quidditch World Cup in Germany. The jersey was inspired by many of the Canada 150 logos, using geometric shapes, in this case triangles, to form the maple leaf.
