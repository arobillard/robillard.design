Polished

SaaS Website

Sumative project for Web Development II and Interaction Design I of Algonquin College's Graphic Design program. The objective was to create a website to advertise a a fictional substriction based service that the designer creates.

Polished is a basic branding and printing service targeted at minor sports teams who rely on volunteers. The website provides examples of products, descriptions of the capabilities of the software, instructions on how to sign up, and different pricing levels.
